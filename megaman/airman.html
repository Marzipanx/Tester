AIR MAN

HP:
Mega Man 2: 28
Mega Man II: 19

Attack Damage:
Mega Man 2: 4 (Air Shooter) 8 (contact) Mega Man II: 2 (Air Shooter) 4 (contact)

Weakness:
Leaf Shield (MM2, MMII, SAR) Super Arm (Power Fighters)

Weapon: Air Shooter

Summary: Air Man (エアーマン Eāman) is a Wily Number who debuted in Mega Man 2. He was built by Dr. Wily specifically for combat in his revenge against Mega Man. Air Man has his face built into his torso instead of a head - an unusual design, said to be intimidating.


Weapon: His Special Weapon is the Air Shooter, which can generate powerful winds with the propeller in his torso - in particular, he is able to push enemies away or pull them or other items towards him, generate typhoons, and attack foes with rapid barrages of tornadoes.

Appearance: Air Man is a stout robot with a blue body, has red eyes at the level of his head and shoulders and has embeded an air fan in the center of the torso. His limbs are black and yellow, and his left arm is an arm cannon with which he uses his Air Shooter.
