HEAT MAN

HP: 28 (Mega Man 2)

Mega Man 2: 4 (Atomic Fire) 8 (contact) 8 (Bodily Crash)

Weakness:Bubble Lead Ice Slasher (Mega Man: The Power Battle)

Weapon: Atomic Fire

Summary: Heat Man (ヒートマン Hītoman) is a character known as a Wily Number from the video game Mega Man 2, designed by Dr. Wily. He is modeled after Fire Man and was specifically constructed for combat as part of Dr. Wily's plan for revenge against Mega Man. Heat Man is characterized by a box-like body that resembles a Zippo lighter.

Weapon: His Special Weapon is the Atomic Fire, a heat-based weapon powered by highly compressed nuclear energy, which can be charged to produce bursts of fire at temperatures of up to 12,000 degrees Celsius (21,632 degrees Fahrenheit). He can also spit fire from his mouth and project enormous fire blasts from his hands. Heat Man surrounds himself in flames for protection, then charges opponents.

Appearance: Heat Man is a humanoid robot with green eyes and yellow eyelids. He has a black body, a yellow box-like armor with a green gem on the front center, a red helmet with a yellow vertical line on top, red pauldrons, red hands and red boots with yellow part on top.
