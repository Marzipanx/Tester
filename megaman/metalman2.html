METAL MAN

HP:
Mega Man 2: 28
Mega Man II: 19
Super Adventure Rockman: 3200

Attack Damage: Mega Man 2: 3 (Metal Blade) 6 (contact) Mega Man II: 4 (contact) 1 (Metal Blade) Super Adventure Rockman: 5 (Metal Blade) 10 (slice)

Weakness: Mega Man 2: Quick Boomerang Metal Blade (2nd encounter) Mega Man II: Crash Bomber Super Adventure Rockman: Mega Buster

Weapon: Metal Blade

Summary: Metal Man (メタルマン Metaruman) is a Wily Number who debuted in Mega Man 2. He was the first Wily Number built by Dr. Wily, created specifically for combat in his revenge against Mega Man. Wily based Metal Man's design on Cut Man.

Weapon: His Special Weapon is the Metal Blade, which are large, razor-sharp, circular saw blades made of ceramic titanium that he can throw at a high speed with deadly accuracy. His lightweight design also grants him high agility, but because of a design flaw, Metal Man is particularly vulnerable to Mega Man's Mega Buster and to his own Metal Blades

Appearance: Metal Man is a humanoid robot of average height with red eyes. His body resembles a black skin tight suit covered by a red armour with yellow accents. His helmet is red and holds a small round saw blade on the forehead and he has yellow knobs with spikes on the sides where his ears are, he also sports a red mask that covers his nose and mouth. His armor is red with a blue trim surrounding a yellow circle on the center of his chest plate and also has two
round saw blades where his shoulders meet his body.
