Summary: Bubble Man (バブルマン Baburuman) is a Wily Number who debuted in Mega Man 2. He is the first aquatic humanoid robot created by Dr. Wily for underwater combat. However, a defect in his systems made him unable to walk on land, being only able to move by jumping (akin to a frog)

Weapon: Bubble Man's Special Weapon, the Bubble Lead, is an offensive and defensive tool that can be fired from his left arm cannon and his head-mounted bubble gun. This weapon allows Bubble Man to attack opponents with small bursts of bubbles while also enabling him to blow larger, more durable bubbles. These large bubbles can trap opponents or shield Bubble Man.

Appearance: Bubble Man is a rotund robot with dark blue eyes. His body resembles a blue skin tight suit with a dark green armor over. He sports a head set which consists of a helmet diving goggles, a mask that covers the lower half of his face, and a snorkel-like tube in the top of his head that he uses to shoot his Bubble Lead. He also sports a pair of black flippers.
