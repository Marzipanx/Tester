FLASH MAN

HP: Mega Man 2: 28 Mega Man: Dr. Wily's Revenge: 19

Attack Damage:
Mega Man 2: 4 (contact) 0 (Time Stopper) 2 (shots)

Mega Man 2: Metal Blade/Crash Bomber Mega Man: Dr. Wily's Revenge Ice Slasher/Atomic Fire Super Adventure Rockman: Metal Blade

Weapon: Time Stopper

Summary: Flash Man (フラッシュマン Furasshuman) is a Wily Number from Mega Man 2 created by Dr. Wily specifically for combat in his revenge against Mega Man. Specifically, he was made to challenge the everlasting idea of controlling time.

Weapon: His Special Weapon is the Time Stopper, a unique system that allows him to stop time in his vicinity for brief periods. To supplement his attacking abilities, he is also equipped with a rapid-fire buster on his right arm. The eight Wily Numbers from Mega Man 2 have built-in devices that make them immune to the Time Stopper, but Quick Man's device has a flaw, making him vulnerable to it.

Appearance: Flash Man is a humanoid robot with green eyes. He has a yellow body, a blue helmet with a yellow hemisphere on it, a yellow gem on the top front of the helmet, blue armor, sky blue pauldrons with a blue line on the bottom, gray arms, a white buster on the left arm, blue tracer and yellow hands on the right arm, a blue part on the bottom of his body, gray legs, and blue boots with two horizontal lines on each of them.
