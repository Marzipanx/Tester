Summary: Quick Man (クイックマン Kuikkuman) is a Wily Number who debuted in Mega Man 2. He was created by Dr. Wily specifically for combat in his revenge against Mega Man using Elec Man's design as a base. His body is made of lightweight materials, allowing him to move at very high speeds, but this can easily cause him to be blown away by weapons like Air Man's Air Shooter.

Weapon: His special weapon, the Quick Boomerang, consists of small, razor-edged boomerangs that he can quickly shoot from a launcher on his right wrist. In various adaptations, such as Super Adventure Rockman, he has also displayed the ability to wield a larger boomerang in a sword-like fashion or to utilize an arm cannon attached to his right arm.

Appearance: Quick Man is a humanoid robot who seemingly wears a black suit, a red torso with a yellow boomerang on front of it, red hands, red leg armors, red feet, and red helmet with a long yellow boomerang on the front. His eyes are blue.
