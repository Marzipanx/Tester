CRASH MAN

HP: 28 (MM2) 19 (MMII)

Mega Man 2: 4 (contact) 4 (Crash Bomber) 4 (Crash Bomber explosion)

Air Shooter (Mega Man 2, Mega Man II, Super Adventure Rockman) Rolling Cutter (Mega Man: The Power Battle)

Weapon: Crash Bomber

Crash Man, known as Clash Man (クラッシュマン Kurasshuman) in Japan, is a Wily Number who debuted in Mega Man 2. Crash Man was built by Dr. Wily specifically for combat in his revenge against Mega Man using the designs of Bomb Man and Guts Man as a base, with high speed and agility, the use of high explosives as primary weapons, and clad in a thick armor that can effectively withstand explosions.

His Special Weapon is the Crash Bomber, powerful time bombs that attach themselves to their targets before detonating. Crash Man simply walks back and forth along the arena, though he turns around just before reaching the wall. If the player shoots, Crash Man jumps high with variable speed (in the player’s general direction) and launches a Crash Bomb at the player on the way down.

Appearance: Crash Man is a humanoid robot with green eyes. He has a yellow torso, white limbs, red armor with a large green orb on the front, red forearms with Crash Bombers, red boots with yellow knees, and a red helmet with a white protrusion and yellow details.
