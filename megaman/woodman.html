WOOD MAN

HP: 28 (MM2), 19 (MMII), 40 (MM8, Sega Saturn)

Attack Damage: Mega Man 2: 8 (Leaf Shield) 4 (leaves) 8 (contact) Mega Man 8: Sega Saturn Version: 6 (contact) 5 (Leaf Shield) 4 (leaves)

Weakness: Atomic Fire (MM2, MM:TPB, SAR) Air Shooter (MM2) Metal Blade (MMII) Flame Sword (MM8, Sega Saturn) Triple Blade (MM10)

Weapon: Leaf Shield

Summary: Wood Man (ウッドマン Uddoman) is a Wily Number created by Dr. Wily specifically for combat in his revenge against Mega Man. He has a very unique design due to his body being made almost completely out of natural Japanese cypresses- including his internal mechanisms, along with a thin coating of metal for extra protection.

Weapon: His Special Weapon is the Leaf Shield, leaf-shaped pieces of Ceratanium that are sharpened around the edges and levitated using electromagnetism that can be used to protect himself and attack enemies in different ways, including launching them in rings of four or eight and summoning them down from the ceiling to fall down onto the target.

Appearance:Wood Man is a bulky humanoid robot with blue, or dark green eyes. Color varies by sprite and official art. He has a gray lower body with a brown, wooden upper torso. He has a wooden stump helmet with a small branch on the right side of it, wooden tracer, tan hands and wooden boots.
